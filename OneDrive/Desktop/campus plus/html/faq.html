Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Typically, it takes 3-5 business days for domestic shipping and 7-14 business days for international shipping.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes, we offer 24/7 customer support. You can contact us via email, phone, or live chat at any time.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website.
